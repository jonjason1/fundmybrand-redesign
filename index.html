What is OPM, and why does it change everything?

OPM stands for Other People's Money. Instead of grinding to save capital or giving up equity, you build a business credit profile that unlocks access to $50,000–$150,000 in funding — using the same financial system large companies have always used.

Most entrepreneurs don't know this system exists. Fund My Brand teaches you to navigate it step by step, with AI cutting weeks off every stage.

The framework

9 steps from zero credit to $100K+ funded

Every step is sequenced deliberately. Skip one and the next doesn't work. Follow all nine and the system does.

  1. 1

    Establish a Fundable Business Structure

    Set up the legal entity and address infrastructure that lenders and credit bureaus recognize as a legitimate, fundable company.

  2. 2

    Form Your Corporation Strategically

    Incorporate in the right state with the right structure to maximize business credit eligibility from day one.

  3. 3

    Secure Your Federal EIN

    Obtain your Employer Identification Number — the business equivalent of a Social Security Number — to open accounts and build a credit profile separate from your personal credit.

  4. 4

    Activate Your Business Banking Infrastructure

    Open the right business bank accounts at the right institutions to establish a verifiable financial history lenders require before extending credit.

  5. 5

    Elevate Your Business Credit Profile

    Register with Dun & Bradstreet, Equifax Business, and Experian Business. Seed your profile with net-30 vendor accounts that report positive payment history.

  6. 6

    Strengthen Key Banking Relationships

    Build consistent account behaviors and banking history — the signals lenders use to determine creditworthiness for larger credit lines.

  7. 7

    Develop Your Funding Strategy

    Map your credit stacking sequence — which instruments to pursue, in what order, and in what amounts to reach your $100K+ target without triggering denials.

  8. 8

    Explore High-Value Funding Options

    Identify the specific business credit cards, lines of credit, and 0% intro APR instruments available to your profile — and apply in the proven optimal sequence.

  9. 9

    Master the Application Process

    Use AI tools to prepare applications, respond to lender requests, and manage documentation — compressing months of work into weeks.

Common questions

Before you get the book

What is OPM and how does it apply to my brand?

OPM (Other People's Money) is a funding strategy where you access capital through business credit lines, 0% intro APR cards, and institutional credit instruments — not personal savings or investors. Fund My Brand shows you how to build and access this system step by step.

Do I need an existing business or revenue?

No. The framework is designed for founders at the 0–2 year stage. You'll learn how to establish the exact business structures lenders look for, even without revenue history or an established track record.

Will this affect my personal credit score?

The framework is built around business credit — a separate system from your personal credit bureau. When structured correctly, business credit approvals do not create hard inquiries on your personal report.

Is $100K+ a guarantee?

No. Results depend on your business structure, credit history, lender criteria at the time of application, and how consistently you follow the framework. $100K+ is the target the system is designed around. Individual results will vary — see our full disclaimer below.
